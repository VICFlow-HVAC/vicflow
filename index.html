FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most from Victorian homeowners.

How much does split system installation cost in Victoria? +

Installation costs typically range from $300 to $800+ depending on the unit size, location and any additional electrical work required. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your home.

Do you service all areas of Victoria? +

Yes! We cover Melbourne metropolitan areas, outer suburbs, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo and most regional Victoria locations. Call us to confirm coverage for your area.

Should I repair or replace my air conditioner? +

If your system is over 10-12 years old and requires significant repairs, replacement is often more cost-effective. Our technicians will always give you an honest assessment and let you decide.

How often should heating and cooling be serviced? +

We recommend annual servicing for all systems — ideally before the summer and winter seasons. Regular maintenance extends system life, improves efficiency and prevents costly breakdowns.

Do you service rental properties? +

Absolutely. We work with landlords, property managers and tenants across Victoria. We can liaise with all parties to minimise disruption and keep the property compliant with safety requirements.

Can I get a quick estimate via photo? +

Yes! Send us photos of your existing system or the area where you need installation and we can provide a fast estimate without needing a site visit for many standard jobs.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes. All our technicians are fully licensed, ARC authorised for refrigerant handling, and we carry full public liability insurance. Your home and family are protected with every job.

What warranty do you offer? +

We offer up to 7 years warranty on select installations. Manufacturer warranties apply to all equipment, plus our own workmanship guarantee. Ask us for details when you get your quote.
